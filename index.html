Lorelai Gilmore

About Lorelai

Lorelai is an amazing and brave woman who has overcome hardship. She got pregnant at 16 and raised her daughter on her own.

Lorelai and Rory

Lorelai's Characteristics

  1. She's Executive Manager of the Independence Inn.
  2. She is independent herself.
  3. She craves a relationship with her parents even though they drive her crazy.
  4. Her relationship with her daughter is enviable.

Lorelai's Friends

Lorelai has some amazing friends. She is best friends with Sookie St. James. She's also friends with Luke Danes, the town's diner owner, and some other townspeople.
